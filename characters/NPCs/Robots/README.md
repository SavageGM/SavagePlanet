# Robots

Physical robotic NPCs, synthetic machines, drones, androids, and autonomous mechanical units for Savage Planet.

Artificial-intelligence characters are classified as NPCs, not robots. A standalone AI character belongs in `characters/NPCs/Individuals/`, while an AI associated with a faction or organization belongs in the appropriate folder under `characters/NPCs/Groups/`.

An AI that operates a robotic body is filed according to the character's identity and affiliation, not merely the body it inhabits.
